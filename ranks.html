Ranks and Perks

We offer a variety of ranks that provide special benefits and perks! Here is a breakdown of the different ranks:

  • Rank 1 (Member): Access to basic server features.
  • Rank 2 (VIP): Extra perks like special chat colors and priority access to certain areas.
  • Rank 3 (Admin): Full administrative privileges including server moderation.

Each rank offers something unique to enhance your experience. Check out our shop to upgrade your rank!
